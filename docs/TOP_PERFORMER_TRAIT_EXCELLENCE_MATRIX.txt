     - Retain top performers: career path, peer coaching incentives, recognition

  6. Behavioral Profiling
     - Document top agents' listening techniques, tone patterns, customer rapport
     - Codify into training modules, QA rubrics, role-play scenarios
     - Continuous improvement based on emerging patterns

EXPECTED OUTCOME:
