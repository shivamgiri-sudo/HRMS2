# HRMS2 Role Wise Activity Flow

## HR

Reviews candidate form, uploaded documents, BGV status, DPDP consent, and onboarding blockers.

## Payroll HR

Validates DOJ, salary effective date, payroll month, salary slab, proposal salary, statutory fields, reporting manager, department, process, cost centre, and salary register lock.

## Branch Head

Approves offer/salary proposal where branch approval is required.

## Operations

Approves proposal salary exceptions and confirms process/operational fit.

## Finance

Final approval for salary exceptions before salary register lock.

## JCLR Coordinator

Confirms joining location, system, ID card, training batch, transport, and induction readiness.

## IT/Admin Provisioning

Acts after readiness and employee creation, using provisioning tabs/routes already present in HRMS2.
